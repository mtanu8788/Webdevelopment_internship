About

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India's missile and nuclear programs and inspired millions with his vision.

Achievements

  • Known as the "Missile Man of India"
  • 11th President of India (2002–2007)
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna
  • Contributed to ISRO and DRDO missions
